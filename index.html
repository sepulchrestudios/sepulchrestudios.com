About

Professional Audio Excellence since 2024

Founded in 2024, Sepulchre Studios was built on a simple mission: to provide creators, artists, bands, and brands with world-class sonics without the gatekeeping. We combine decade-honed technical expertise with a passion for storytelling to deliver audio that stands out in a crowded market.

From precision mixing to immersive sound design, we treat every project as a partnership. Whether you're an artist or a filmmaker, we are here to ensure your vision is heard with clarity, power, and professional polish.

Let's make something incredible together.

Matthew Fritz - Engineer / Mixer / Artist / Owner

Matthew's journey in sound began in the fast-paced world of broadcast radio in 2006, where he mastered the art of capturing clean, impactful audio under pressure. Over the years, he's transitioned that foundation into a career defined by technical precision and creative mentorship.

Beyond the desk, he's had the privilege of mentoring aspiring engineers, helping them navigate the complexities of signal flow and acoustics. He combines experience with a passion for helping others find their unique sound, both in-the-box and out-of-the-box.
